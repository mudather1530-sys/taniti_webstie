Why Visit Taniti?

Taniti is a small tropical island paradise in the Pacific with an area of less than 500 square miles. Our varied terrain includes sandy and rocky beaches, a safe harbor, lush tropical rainforests, and a mountainous interior with a small active volcano.

20,000 Friendly Locals
500 Square Miles of Beauty
365 Days of Sunshine

Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or cultural experiences, Taniti offers the perfect tropical escape. Our island combines natural beauty with modern amenities, making it an ideal destination for families, couples, and solo travelers alike.
