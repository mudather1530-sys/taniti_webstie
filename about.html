Welcome to Taniti

Taniti is a small, tropical island paradise in the Pacific Ocean. With an area of less than 500 square miles, our island offers an intimate yet diverse experience for every traveler.

Our varied terrain makes Taniti truly special:

  • Sandy & Rocky Beaches: Pristine white sand beaches surround Yellow Leaf Bay
  • Safe Harbor: Protected bay perfect for boating and water activities
  • Lush Rainforests: Tropical forests teeming with exotic wildlife
  • Mountainous Interior: Dramatic landscape with hiking trails
  • Active Volcano: Small, safe, and fascinating geological wonder

üë• People & Culture

Taniti has an indigenous population of about 20,000 friendly locals who are proud to share their island with visitors.

Until recently, our economy was dominated by fishing and agriculture. Tourism has grown, but we maintain our traditional way of life and cultural heritage. Many younger Tanitians speak English, and our multilingual hospital staff ensures everyone feels welcome.

üåÜ Key Areas

Taniti City: Our charming capital features native architecture and serves as the cultural and commercial heart of the island. Most tourists spend time here exploring shops, restaurants, and nearby beaches.

Merriton Landing: A rapidly developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay. This entertainment district offers pubs, restaurants, activities, and nightlife. It's walkable and perfect for exploring on foot.

Yellow Leaf Bay: Our picturesque harbor surrounded by pristine beaches, offering swimming, water sports, and breathtaking sunsets.
